Children's Privacy Protection Guidelines

Last update: June 1, 2024
Effective date: June 1, 2024

PikaBoo Studio places immense value on personal information and takes all possible measures to protect your personal information. Besides our Privacy Policy and User Agreement, we have also established the Children's Privacy Protection Guidelines ("the current policy") to further help you and your child ("child" or "children" hereinafter refers to minors who are under the age of 15) familiarize yourselves with the ways how PikaBoo Studio collects, uses, stores, or transfers your personal information, as well as the rights you and your child enjoy while using PikaBoo Studio's products or services.

Attention

The current policy is applicable to the personal information protection of minors who are under 15 years of age. If you are under the age of 15, please get the approval from your guardian/parent before using our products or service.

1. How PikaBoo Studio collects children's personal information

1.1

When your child uses PikaBoo Studio's products or services, his/her personal information that is collected is limited only to the information which you permit us to collect or the information that you and your child provide voluntarily, and such collection is done to provide and optimize the services we provide to your child, and to ensure account security. For more details of the types of personal information we may collect about your child, please refer to the first part of the Privacy Policy.

1.2

To confirm the custodial relationship between you and your child, we may need your identification, contact information, and other relevant information, which will not be used for any other purposes except for the confirmation and can be deleted at your request. We may need your authentication credentials, and once these are submitted, we will not be able to verify until you submit proof except for the confirmation and can be deleted at your request. Contact details for deleting the information is listed in section 4.

2. How PikaBoo Studio uses children's personal information

2.1

With the aim of protecting children's personal information, our use of such information will strictly comply with the relevant law and regulations, your authorization, and the terms of the current policy, User Agreement, and Privacy Policy.

2.2

For a more detailed description of how we use children's personal information, please refer to our Privacy Policy. If there is a need to use such information for any purpose or function other than stipulated during its collection, we will notify you and ask for your consent before we do so.

3. The safety of children's personal information

3.1

PikaBoo Studio will only retain your child's personal information for the period necessary for the purpose described in the current policy, or as required by relevant law and regulations.

3.2

In view of the fact that the internet is not 100% safe, PikaBoo Studio will try its best to ensure or guarantee the security of any information you send us. In the rare unfortunate event of personal information leakage, we will notify you at first, as required by law of the basic information of that leakage, its possible effect, the measures that have been taken or will be taken by PikaBoo Studio, the advice for you to prevent or lower future risks by yourself, and our compensation to you if any.

4. Editing and deleting children's personal information

4.1

To edit or delete the personal information of your child, you can contact our customer service through telephone or in-game message. We will confirm the custodial relationship between you and your child before deleting any information.
